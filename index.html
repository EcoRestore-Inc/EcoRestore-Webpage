We’re bringing new tools to help restore old Florida, one piece of land at a time.

About EcoRestore

EcoRestore is a nonprofit initiative using science and technology to restore Florida’s landscapes with native plants, data-driven tools, and community partnerships.

What We're Building

  • Plant ID & Native Replacement Guidance
  • Mapping & Restoration Journal
  • Network of Nurseries & Professionals
  • Future Tools: Prescribed Fire, Invasive Species Management, Drone Monitoring

Our Partnerships

EcoRestore is actively building collaborations with nurseries, agencies, and conservation networks.

  • Florida Association of Native Nurseries (FANN)
  • Florida Native Plant Society (FNPS)
  • University of Florida - College of Agricultural and Life Sciences (CALS)
  • University of Florida - CISE Department
  • University of Florida - Warrington College of Business
