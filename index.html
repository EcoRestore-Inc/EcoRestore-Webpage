About EcoRestore

EcoRestore is a nonprofit initiative and technology platform dedicated to restoring Florida’s landscapes to native ecosystems. We connect people, science, and technology to make ecological restoration accessible, engaging, and impactful for all.

What We're Building

  • Plant identification & native replacement guidance
  • Restoration journal & engagement features
  • Mapping tools for small yards to large parcels
  • Community hub for nurseries and restoration services
  • Future: Drone-enabled aerial monitoring

Partnerships

EcoRestore collaborates with University of Florida (CALS, CISE, Warrington College of Business) and is actively engaging with local nurseries. EcoRestore maintains a curated network of native plant nurseries that spans across Florida. This network draws from public directories (including FANN, FNPS, and other conservation partners), as well as direct outreach to nurseries, ensuring coverage in every region of the state.

EcoRestore Community Resource Hub

A living network of nurseries and resources that support ecological restoration across Florida. This hub connects communities with sources of native plants and restoration knowledge.
