Mission

EcoRestore is a nonprofit initiative helping Floridians restore native landscapes through technology, science, and community action.

What We’re Building

🌿 Plant ID & Native Replacements
Identify invasives and suggest native alternatives.
📍 Mapping & GIS Tools
Visualize parcels and plan restoration steps.
📓 Restoration Journal
Track activities, progress, and outcomes.
🔥 Prescribed Fire Guidance
Best practices and planning resources.
🚁 (Future) Drone & AI
Aerial imagery and detection overlays.

Partners & Collaborations

FNPS chapters · FANN · University of Florida (CALS, Warrington, CISE) · Prescribed Fire Councils
